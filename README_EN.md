**Holy Read** is a Bible reading and study application designed for the modern reader. It combines minimalist design, advanced interactive animations, and powerful features to provide the purest and smartest spiritual meditation experience.

## ‚ú® Core Features

- **Multi-lingual Support**: Built-in **English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese** versions with seamless one-tap switching for both UI and scriptures.
- **Global Search**: A powerful search engine to find keywords across the entire Bible in real-time, featuring keyword highlighting and instant location jumping.
- **Smart TTS (Text-to-Speech)**:
  - **Continuous Playback**: Automatically advances to the next verse, supporting continuous reading through entire books.
  - **Auto-Scroll**: Follows the spoken text automatically, keeping the active verse in your view.
  - **Adjustable Speed**: Control playback rate from 0.5x to 2.0x.
- **Fullscreen Reader Mode**:
  - Multiple layout modes including **Page Flip, Smooth Slide, and Vertical Scroll**.
  - Minimalist UI to eliminate distractions for deep reading sessions.
- **Advanced Study Tools**:
  - **Range-based Highlights**: Highlight single verses or ranges with multiple colors.
  - **Range-based Bookmarks**: Save specific passages and search through your bookmarks locally.
  - **Spiritual Notes**: Record insights with a real-time searchable interface and batch editing support.
- **Deep Customization**:
  - **Theme System**: Light, Dark, and Sepia modes.
  - **Visual Elements**: Customize background colors and accent colors to match your preference.
  - **Typography**: Adjust font size, line height, and font families (Serif, Sans, Kaiti, Rounded).
